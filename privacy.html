UrbanAuraHub Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this application collects, uses, and protects information in connection with its use of Pinterest API data and other publicly available trend data sources.

1. Purpose of the Application

This application is an internal research and analytics tool used to identify emerging product, design, and merchandising opportunities for print-on-demand listings.

2. Information We Collect

This application does not collect or sell sensitive personal information from end users. If Pinterest API data is accessed, it is used only to retrieve trend-related keywords, topics, and analytics made available through Pinterest’s developer platform.

3. How We Use Information

We use information only to analyze trend signals, identify emerging product and design opportunities, improve internal research workflows, and maintain application security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data and do not share collected information with third parties except as necessary to operate, secure, or maintain the application, or as required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

Any credentials, access tokens, and related technical data are stored securely on the server side and are not exposed publicly.
